How to insert this into Adobe InDesign:

  1. Open your Adobe InDesign document.
  2. Press Ctrl + D (Windows) or Cmd + D (Mac) to open the Place dialog.
  3. Select the downloaded Exported_Matched_Images.pdf file.
  4. Check "Show Import Options" at the bottom of the dialog if you want to select specific images (pages).
  5. Click anywhere on your document layout to place the images!
